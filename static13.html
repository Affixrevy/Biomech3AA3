Static Equilibrium of Rigid Bodies (2-D)

Sample Problem:

Using the diagram of the right arm of the gymnast, find the magnitude of the muscle force (M) and the joint reaction forces at the shoulder.

Solution (continued)

Resolving the force of gravity into X and Y components.

The magnitude of the gravitational force is always equal to the mass of the object multiplied by the gravitational constant of 9.81. This force always acts directly downward (negative Y component) and has a zero X component.
