Rectangular Components of Vectors

When forces and other vectors are resolved, they generally are resolved into two components at right angles to each other, ie. the rectangular components of a force. This makes things rather easy because we are always dealing with right angle triangles where Pythagorous and simple trigonometry are all that is necessary.
