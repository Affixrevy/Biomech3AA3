Question 19

The "tire flip" exercise is a good whole body resistance exercise. The proper technique begins with the athlete lifting one side of the tire by lifting upward and also pushing forward. What is the magnitude of her lifting force (F) if the tire has a mass of 275 kg, a diameter of 1.31 m and the direction of her lift is 15o from vertical? (Assume the mass is located in the middle of the tire)

Answer Section:

Correct!
The answer is 1397 N.
