Question 16

A dog walker can walk four dogs by holding their leashes in one hand. She arranges the largest dog in the middle who pulls straight ahead. She places the medium dog to the left and the two small dogs to the right of the large dog. The magnitude of the force of each dog is 50 N, 25 N, 15 N, and 15 N for the largest to smallest, respectfully. Given the directions of pull in the diagram, what is the net force in the Right-Left direction (Left is positive and Right is negative)?
