Question 14

The masseter muscle can generate a biting force (Freaction) by creating a moment of force about the axis of the jaw. In this case, the muscle generated a biting force (Freaction) of 100 N, How much would that force be reduced if the bite was applied 2.0 cm more in the anterior direction?
