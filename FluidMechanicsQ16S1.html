Question 16

A scientists wished to know the density of a blood sample. When he places a drop of the blood in pure Xylene that has a density 0.867 kg/l, the drop sank to the bottom of the beaker. When he placed a drop of the blood in pure Bromobenzene that has a density of 1.497 kg/l, the drop floated on the surface. When he created a mixture of 69% Xylene and 31% Bromobenzene, the drop remained suspended without sinking or floating, What was the density of the blood sample?
