Moment of Force / (Torque)

Example: Find the moment of force caused by the Biceps muscle about the elbow axis.

There are three steps to calculating the moment of force.

Step 1 - find the magnitude of the force (F) and the length of the moment arm (MA).

Step 2 - solve M = F x MA

Step 3 - use the right-hand rule to determine the direction
