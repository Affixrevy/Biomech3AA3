Question 14

An iron weight with the dimensions of 0.1 x 0.1 m x 0.1 m is placed in the middle at the bottom of the box. If the density of the iron weight is 7800 kg/m3, how far does the box sink into the fresh water (h)?

Answer Section:

Correct!
The answer is 0.0741 m.
