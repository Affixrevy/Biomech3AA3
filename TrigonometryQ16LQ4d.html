Question 16

A Hockey player is directly in front of the goal and 5.0 m away. The width of the goal is 1.8 m and the goaltender blocks 0.86m (green zone). In figure A, the goaltender is on the goal line and the shooter must shoot the puck through the red zone to the left or right of the goaltender in order to score. By what percentage has the goaltender reduced the angle (red zone) by moving forward by 2.0 m (figure B)?
