Question 8

A hockey stick traveling forward at 30 m/s strikes a puck also traveling forward at 5 m/s. The mass of the stick is 2.0 kg. the mass of the puck is 0.09 kg, and the coefficient of restitution is 0.7. What is the momentum of the puck after impact?

Solution Section:

The conservation of momentum requires the sum of the momentum of the stick and puck before the impact to equal the sum of the momentum of the stick and puck after the impact.
