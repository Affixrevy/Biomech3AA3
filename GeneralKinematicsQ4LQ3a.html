Question 4

What is the angular velocity of a limb if its angle changes from 20 degrees below the positive X axis to 18 degrees above the positive X axis in two adjacent frames of video if the frame rate was 30 frames per second?

Answer Section:

  • 1
  • 2
  • 3

Correct. If you are careful when you enter these values into your calculator, you should get the correct answer.

Solution Section:

The average velocity is equal to the change in position (Δθ)  divided by the change in time (Δt). We previously calculated the change in position to be 38 degrees in the positive direction and the change in time is 1/30 of a second due to the 30 frame per second video frame rate.
