Question 8

A weightlifter performing a squat exercise raises and lowers a 100 kg bar 8 times in one minute. If the distance of each lift is 1 m, what is the average power produced? (use only the positive work of lifting the weight)

Solution Section:

Work is a change in energy. In this case, the weightlifter increased the potential energy of the weight by lifting it to a height of 1 meter, 8 times. The mass of the weight was 100 kg. We calculate this change in potential energy by multiplying the mass (100 kg) by the gravitational constant (9.81 m/s2) and the height (8 m).
