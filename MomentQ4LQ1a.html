Solution Section:

The moment of force is the effectiveness of a force to produce rotation. It is calculated as the product of the force and the moment arm. The moment arm is the perpendicular distance between the imaginary line that represents the line of action or direction of the force and the axis of rotation. In this case, F = 100 N and the moment arm is 0.042 m.. The resulting moment i2 4.2 N.m and the moment is negative according to the "right hand rule".
