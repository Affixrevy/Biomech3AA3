Solution Section:

We previously used the sum of the moments on the pulley and free body diagrams of the blocks to get expressions of the tension forces on either side of the pulley, as well as  the tangential acceleration formula to equate the linear and angular accelerations. We can now write an equation  of the sum of the moments with the linear acceleration (a) as the only unknown. We isolate a on one side of the equation by itself and solve. Finally, we use the tangential acceleration formula again to convert the linear acceleration (a) into an angular acceleration of the pulley (α).
