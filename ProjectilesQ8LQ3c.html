Question 8

A quarterback throws a football at an unknown angle with respect to the horizontal. If the height of release and catch is 2.3 m above the ground, the total time of flight is 3.5 s, and the horizontal distance from ball (at release) to high point (HP) is 27.0 m, at what angle did the quarterback release the ball?

Solution Section:

Since the ball is caught at the same height as it was thrown, the projectile motion is a symmetrical parabola with the maximum height exactly in the middle. This means that the ball spends half of the time reaching maximum height and will have a horizontal distance of 27 m.
