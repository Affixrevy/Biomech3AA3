Question 7

Two blocks are initially at rest with m1 sitting on top of m2. The mass of m1 is 10 kg and the mass of m2 is 20 kg. The coefficient of static friction between the floor and m2 is 0.4 and the coefficient of kinetic friction between the floor and m2 is 0.2. The coefficient of static friction between m1 and m2 is 0.7 and the coefficient of kinetic friction between m1 and m2 is 0.6. A force of 70 N is applied horizontally to m1. What is the acceleration of m2?

Answer Section:

Correct!
The answer is 0 m/s2.
